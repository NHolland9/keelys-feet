Holistic Treatments

As well as providing foot healthcare services I am also qualified in the following holistic treatments.

Reflexology

Reflexology is the application of pressure on certain points on the feet which correspond to different areas of the body in order to heal these areas. Reflexology is a very individual treatment which is tailored to you as a whole, taking into account many factors which might be affecting your wellbeing. Reflexology can also increase energy and circulation, promote a healthy immune system, relieve stress, sleep disorders, depression, pain and many more. Many have monthly appointments though if you have a specific health problem to address a 6 week course of weekly appointments is recommended. Reflexology should not be used to diagnose disease or as an alternative to medical advice

Back, Neck & Shoulder Massage

A popular massage that focuses on the build-up of stress and muscle tension, the back, neck, and shoulder massage is particularly good for those who spend long periods of time sitting at their desk. The back, neck, and shoulder massage relaxes muscles, relieves stress, improves circulation, and encourages general relaxation. It is also good for relieving tension headaches, improving flexibility and can help to strengthen the immune system.

Full Body Swedish Massage

Swedish massage is the western standard for massage. The various techniques are designed to improve your circulation, soothe your muscles and make you feel more relaxed. Besides the calming benefits, Swedish massage is also useful for easing muscular strain by flushing out toxins, helping to keep ligaments and tendons supple, and reducing emotional and physical stress.
