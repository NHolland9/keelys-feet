EXPERT FOOT CARE SINCE 2012

Thetford-based foot healthcare practitioner providing treatments in the comfort of your own home

Keely Holland DipCFHP, MPSPract.

An accredited Foot Health Practioner (MPSPract) and holder of an advanced diploma in Foot Health Practice (DipCFHP), I have provided quality foot care in and around Thetford since 2012. Specialising in home visits (I also take group bookings at care homes) I offer treatments for all foot ailments, from toenail trimming and hard skin removal to diabetic foot care, and I'm also trained in several holistic therapies including reflexology and massage. I'm always looking for new clients so if your feet need some attention get in touch today.

All Feet Catered For

Young or old we can all develop problems with our feet at any age, and when we do it's important to care for them sooner rather than later. Many struggle with regular maintenance of the feet and this can lead to more serious issues. As a foot healthcare practioner I am able to perform the necessary maintenance or address any problem areas you might have, helping you get back on your feet in no time.

Treatments at Home

For many with foot ailments getting out of the house can be difficult, especially if walking is hindered or painful. My service includes stress-free home visits as standard allowing you to be at ease in a familiar and comfortable environment during your treatment (and even enjoy a cup of tea!).
